# Stoichiometric Acid Consumption – Chalcopyrite Leaching

## Basis
- Concentrate throughput: 227 t/day
- Copper grade in concentrate: 25% Cu
- Copper to leaching: 56.7 t Cu/day

## Reaction Basis
CuFeS₂ + 4 H₂SO₄ + O₂ → CuSO₄ + FeSO₄ + 4 H₂O + 2 S⁰

## Stoichiometric Calculation

Copper moles:
56,700 kg / 63.55 kg/kmol ≈ 892 kmol Cu/day

Sulfuric acid requirement:
4 × 892 ≈ 3,568 kmol H₂SO₄/day

Corresponding acid mass:
3,568 × 98 ≈ 350 t H₂SO₄/day

## Remarks
This value represents the minimum stoichiometric acid requirement.
Additional acid will be required to account for gangue reactions,
acid losses, and desired free acid concentration in the leach solution.
